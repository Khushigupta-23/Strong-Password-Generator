  1. Ensure the password is at least 6 characters long, and no longer than 20 characters.
  2. Include at least 1 uppercase letter (A-Z), 1 lowercase letter (a-z), 1 number (0-9), and 1 special character (!@#$%^&*).
  3. Avoid using repeated characters (e.g. aaaa, 1111).

  6. Password Strength :-
    • Weak: Only 1-2 character types.
    • Medium: 3 character types.
    • Strong: All 4 character types.
  7. Don't include personal information like name or birthdate.
